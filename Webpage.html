Our Vision & Mission Statements

Our Vision

The child is the father or mother of a man and should be guarded to be so. We see children of all races at every location with opportunities to excel Whether handicapped or priviledged.

Our Mission

We are set up to give succor to orphans and vulnerable children by assisting them and insisting that they should obtain all their rights as advantage UN convention on the Right of Child (C R C) to believe in themselves that they can be singled out from the crowd.
